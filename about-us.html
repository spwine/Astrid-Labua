Our Story

Innovating Partnerships: Our Story as Brand Allies

Innovating Partnerships: Our Story as Brand Allies

Astrid Labua are a factory that produces skincare brands, offering high-quality cosmetics of all kinds to meet customer needs. We prioritize the quality of the products we market, selecting premium raw materials from leading suppliers worldwide. Our process involves research and the creation of new product formulas by cosmetic experts. We pay meticulous attention to every step, from understanding customer requirements to the manufacturing process, to ensure we deliver products that meet quality standards and are safe for consumers.

Vision

Astrid Labua is committed to maintaining and enhancing the quality system of our health and beauty products to achieve international standards of efficiency. We have confidence in our team, our services, and the quality system that our company provides to our customers.

Mission

We aim to become a high-quality manufacturing factory, continuously developing health and beauty products to meet customer needs by sourcing the best and highest quality products. Our goal is to ensure maximum customer satisfaction by conducting business fairly and sustainably.

Our Standard

We are proud to have been certified with ASEAN GMP and ISO 22716 GMP for Cosmetics, having passed quality inspections and received certification. These manufacturing standards are recognized internationally.

Astrid Labua provides every client with high-quality, innovative, and effective cosmetic product solutions that not just exceeds expectations but also meets specific needs.
